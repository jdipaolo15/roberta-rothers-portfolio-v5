Opioids in America

To continue the medical research within the GIS field, I created a Arc Story Map about the opioid issue in America. From discussing the issue to demonstrating who is being affected, I created multiple maps in this project to show each age group that has died due to the effects of opioids in America and what the top 20 cities in America are with the highest opioid levels. The image below is one out of many maps made to indicate those that have OD on opiods between the ages of 25-34 to the total amount of ODs in America.
